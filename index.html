Services

Personal shopping

As a former boutique owner, I'm a lifelong supporter of local small retailers & love to support my community. I'll start local and then fill in the holes online by pulling from my resources of hundreds of designers also helping with receiving and returning things that don't work.

Closet Edits

I'll purge, organize and re-purpose your closet. We will make lists to fill in gaps and key pieces needed and also alter old pieces you love to make them work for today's trends. We will make your closet easier to maneuver, making getting dressed exciting, not annoying.

Photoshoots

Whether yo're a brand, mag, designer, or just someone who want to take some rad pics, I'll styke fun, interesting, unexpected shoots.

Event & Costumes

This is my creative funky side where I will either find great pieces for you or pull them from personal inventory. As a burner, I've been collecting funky "costumed" outfits for 10+ years. Whether it's leather & lace, Studio 54, 80s prom or exotic birds, I've gotcha.

Side Hustle

Come check out my upcycled collection of jackets at The Story in downtown Santa Barbara. The collection features big and little (think Mommy and Me but add diddes and make it cooler) as well as other repuspoed, re-imagined and re-worked vintage pieces.

Consignment & Vintage

After purging your closet, I'l divide the edited items and send them off to one of my consignment partners so you can realize some $$ back for new goodis. I also keep a highly curated and ever changing collection of pristine vintage pieces at my downtown location (called "The Story").
