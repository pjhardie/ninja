Services

Personal shopping

As a former boutique owner, I'm a lifelong supporter of local retailers & I love to support my community. I'll start by shopping local and then fill in the gaps online by pulling from my resources of current designers. I'll also help with setting up online accounts, receiving shipments and shipping back clothing that doesnt work.

Closet Edits

I'll edit, organize and re-purpose your closet. We will make lists to fill in gaps, adding key pieces needed to complete your closet. I'll suggest how to alter old pieces you love and send off any edited pieces to a one of many consigners I work with so you can make back a little cash for new goodies. We'll make your closet easier to maneuver, making getting dressed fun again.

Photoshoots

Whether you're a brand, magazine, designer, a group of partygoers or just someone who wants to take some rad pics... I'll style fun, interesting, and unexpected shoots.

Vintage

Come and check out my highly curated inventory of hand picked vintage pieces at my physical location (called "The Story") in downtown Santa Barbara. It will be an ever changing themed collection of unique pieces. Check out IG for the current story. If you cant come in person, you can DM me for prices and I'll ship to you.

Side Hustle

-Also at The Story, I'll have matching sets of Big and Littles (think Mommy and Me but add Dads and make it cooler), as well as other repurposed, re-imagined and re-worked vintage pieces.

Events & Costumes

This is my creative, wild side where I will either find great pieces for you or pull them from personal inventory for your themed event. As a burner, I've been collecting funky, one of a kind "costume" pieces for 10+ years. Whether it's sequins and tulle, Studio 54, 80s prom or exotic birds, I've gotchya.

Experience

As a former owner of two contemporary clothing boutiques I've styled a whole lot of people. My boutiques (called 'Blu') were open for over a decade and provided the foundation for my experience, skill and love of clothing.
