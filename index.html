Experience

As a former owner of two boutiques I've dressed and styled thousands of women. My boutiques (called 'Blu' for those of you that used to shop with me) were open for over a decade and provide the foundation for my experience and skill.

Blu - A Boutique

I was a boutique owner for over a decade with stores in Durango, Colorado and Los Cabos, Mexico. During that time, I dressed thousands of women. Life moved on and when I moved to Santa Barbara with my family, I decided to close the stores and focus on working more closely with clients as a personal stylist.
